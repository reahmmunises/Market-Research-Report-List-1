<p><p>The scanning sonar system market is divided into two main types: single beam and multi-beam scanning sonar systems. </p><p>Single beam systems provide focused, high-resolution data for specific areas, making them suitable for tasks like depth measurement and object detection. In contrast, multi-beam systems emit multiple sonar beams simultaneously, allowing for broader environmental mapping and detailed topographic analysis. Both types serve various applications in marine exploration, underwater construction, and environmental monitoring, with each type offering distinct advantages depending on the requirements of the project.</p></p>
