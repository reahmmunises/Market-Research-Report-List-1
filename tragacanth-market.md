<p><p>The tragacanth market can be categorized into two main types: ribbon form and flake form. Ribbon form tragacanth is characterized by its long, fibrous strips, often preferred for applications requiring flexible binding and viscosity in products like pharmaceuticals and cosmetics. Flake form tragacanth, on the other hand, consists of small, flat pieces that dissolve easily, making it suitable for food and beverage industries. Both forms serve distinct purposes based on their physical properties, influencing their demand across various sectors.</p></p>
