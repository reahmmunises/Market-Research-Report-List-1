<p><p>The channel infusion pumps market is segmented into single-channel and multi-channel infusion pumps. Single-channel infusion pumps are designed to deliver medication through one line, making them ideal for simple therapies or patients requiring a single drug. In contrast, multi-channel infusion pumps feature multiple lines, enabling the concurrent administration of several medications or fluids. This versatility makes them suitable for more complex treatments in hospital settings, improving efficiency and allowing for precise control over multiple therapies simultaneously.</p></p>
