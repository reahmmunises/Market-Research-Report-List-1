<p><p>Food traceability technology encompasses various methods to track food products throughout the supply chain. Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) and Real-Time Location Systems (RTLS) provide automated data capture and location tracking. Global Positioning System (GPS) enables geographic tracking for logistics. Barcodes facilitate quick scanning and inventory management. Infrared technology allows for contactless data exchange, while biometrics can authenticate personnel handling food. These technologies enhance transparency, safety, and efficiency in food production and distribution.</p></p>
