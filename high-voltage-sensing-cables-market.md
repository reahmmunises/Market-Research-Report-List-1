<p><p>High Voltage Sensing Cables are essential for monitoring electrical systems and ensuring safety. The market comprises two main types: Single-mode Fiber and Multi-mode Fiber cables. Single-mode Fiber cables transmit signals over long distances with minimal loss, making them ideal for extensive high-voltage applications. In contrast, Multi-mode Fiber cables handle shorter distances and support multiple signals simultaneously, offering higher bandwidth in confined areas. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the reliability and efficiency of high-voltage monitoring systems.</p></p>
