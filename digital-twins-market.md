<p><ul><li>Parts Twin</li><li>Product Twin</li><li>Process Twin</li><li>System Twin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital twins refer to virtual replicas of physical entities, and they are categorized into several market types. A Parts Twin focuses on individual components, enabling precise monitoring and maintenance. A Product Twin represents entire products, helping optimize design and performance. A Process Twin models operational workflows, facilitating efficiency improvements and risk management. Lastly, a System Twin encompasses larger systems, providing insights into interactions among various components, thereby enhancing overall system performance and resilience.</p></p>
