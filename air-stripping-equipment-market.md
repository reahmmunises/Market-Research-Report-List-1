<p><ul><li>Sieve Tray System</li><li>Packed Tower System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air stripping equipment is essential for the removal of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) from contaminated water. The sieve tray system uses a series of trays to create a countercurrent flow of air and water, maximizing mass transfer efficiency. In contrast, the packed tower system employs packing materials to increase surface area for gas-liquid interaction, enhancing the stripping process. Both systems are widely used in environmental remediation, with their choice depending on specific application requirements and operational efficiencies.</p></p>
