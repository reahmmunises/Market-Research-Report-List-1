<p><strong>The Radial Piston Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage Pump</li><li>Multi-stage Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radial piston pumps are categorized into single-stage and multi-stage types based on their design and operational capabilities. Single-stage pumps consist of one piston chamber, offering high flow rates at moderate pressure, making them suitable for simple applications. In contrast, multi-stage pumps feature multiple piston chambers arranged in series, allowing for higher pressure output while maintaining a compact size. This makes them ideal for demanding applications requiring significant pressure without sacrificing efficiency. Both types serve diverse sectors such as industrial and mobile applications.</p></p>
