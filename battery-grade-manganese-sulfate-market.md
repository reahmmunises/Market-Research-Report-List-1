In contrast, the reduction method employs chemical processes, often involving carbon, to convert manganese oxides into manganese sulfate. Both methods cater to the growing demand for high-quality manganese sulfate in battery manufacturing, particularly for lithium-ion batteries, due to their distinct advantages and production efficiencies.</p></p>
