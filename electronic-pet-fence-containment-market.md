<p><p>The Electronic Pet Fence and Containment Market comprises two primary types: In-Ground Fences and Wireless Fences. In-Ground Fences involve burying a wire around the perimeter of the property, creating a defined boundary that pets are trained to recognize. Wireless Fences, on the other hand, use radio signals to establish a circular boundary without the need for physical wires. Both types aim to enhance pet safety by keeping them within designated areas while offering flexibility and accessibility for pet owners.</p></p>
