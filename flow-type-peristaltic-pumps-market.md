<p><p>Flow-type peristaltic pumps are categorized into fixed speed and variable speed markets based on their operational capabilities. Fixed speed peristaltic pumps operate at a constant flow rate, making them suitable for applications where precise and steady fluid transfer is needed. Conversely, variable speed peristaltic pumps allow for adjustable flow rates, providing flexibility to meet varying process demands. This adaptability is crucial in industries requiring precise dosing or varying throughput, enhancing efficiency and control in fluid handling systems.</p></p>
