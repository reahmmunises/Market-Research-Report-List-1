<p><p>High-speed steel (HSS) drills are essential tools in machining, categorized primarily into straight shank, taper shank, and other types. Straight shank drills feature a uniform diameter along their length, making them compatible with various chuck types for easy use. Taper shank drills have a conical shape at one end, ensuring a secure fit in machine holders, which enhances stability during operation. Other types include specialized designs like step drills or combination drills, tailored for specific applications in metalworking and construction.</p></p>
