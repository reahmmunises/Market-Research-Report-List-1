<p><ul><li>External Thread Fasteners</li><li>Internal Threaded Fasteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial screw fasteners market is primarily categorized into external thread fasteners and internal threaded fasteners. External thread fasteners, such as screws and bolts, are designed to be inserted into pre-drilled holes and provide strong connections by engaging with corresponding components. In contrast, internal threaded fasteners, including nuts and threaded inserts, receive external threads and secure assembled parts tightly together. Both types are essential in various applications across industries, ensuring structural integrity and reliability in assemblies.</p></p>
