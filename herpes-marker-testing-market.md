HSV-1 testing targets the herpes simplex virus type 1, commonly associated with oral herpes but can cause genital infections. HSV-2 testing focuses on herpes simplex virus type 2, primarily linked to genital herpes. HSV-1/HSV-2 testing provides a comprehensive analysis for both types, aiding in accurate diagnosis and management. The choice of test depends on clinical presentation and patient history, influencing treatment and counseling strategies.</p></p>
