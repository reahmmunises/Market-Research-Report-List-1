<p><p>The electrolytic sodium hypochlorite generator market is primarily segmented into two types: brine systems and seawater systems. Brine systems use a concentrated salt solution to produce sodium hypochlorite, making them suitable for locations with access to salt and freshwater. Conversely, seawater systems utilize the natural salinity of seawater, rendering them ideal for coastal areas. Both systems offer advantages in efficiency and cost-effectiveness, facilitating the on-site generation of disinfectants for water treatment and sanitation applications.</p></p>
