<p><strong>The Brake Disk Profile Gauge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wheel Mounted</li><li>Axle Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Brake Disk Profile Gauge market is segmented into two primary types: Wheel Mounted and Axle Mounted gauges. Wheel Mounted gauges are attached directly to the wheel hub, allowing for easy assessment of disk wear and profile without removing the wheel, making them efficient for quick inspections. Axle Mounted gauges, on the other hand, are positioned on the axle, providing a stable and precise measurement of disk thickness and profile across multiple wheels, ideal for more thorough evaluations and maintenance checks.</p></p>
