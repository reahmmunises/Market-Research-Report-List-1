<p><p>The bulk cement market encompasses several types, each serving distinct applications. Portland cement is the most widely used, ideal for general construction. White cement, known for its aesthetic appeal, is used in decorative applications and architectural projects. Hydraulic cement hardens when mixed with water, making it suitable for underwater and moist environments. Alumina cement, characterized by its high heat resistance, is utilized in specialized industries like refractory applications and for structures exposed to high temperatures.</p></p>
