<p><p>The bellows valve market comprises various types, primarily focusing on bellows stop valves and bellows gate valves. Bellows stop valves are designed to provide reliable sealing and leak prevention, making them ideal for applications involving hazardous or corrosive fluids. In contrast, bellows gate valves facilitate flow control by lifting a gate to allow or restrict fluid passage. Both types leverage the unique bellows design, ensuring longevity and durability in demanding environments, thereby driving their demand across industries like oil and gas, pharmaceuticals, and water treatment.</p></p>
