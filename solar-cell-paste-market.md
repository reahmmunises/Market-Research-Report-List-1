<p><p>The solar cell paste market consists of three primary types: Al paste, Ag (front) paste, and Ag (back) paste. Al paste is used for the back side contact of solar cells, facilitating efficient energy conversion. Ag (front) paste is essential for creating the front contact, improving conductivity and light absorption. Ag (back) paste serves as a conductive layer on the rear, optimizing performance. Each type plays a critical role in enhancing solar cell efficiency and overall energy output.</p></p>
