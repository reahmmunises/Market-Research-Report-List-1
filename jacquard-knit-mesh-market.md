<p><p>The Jacquard Knit Mesh market is divided into two primary types: Single Layer Knitted Jacquard Mesh and Dual Layer Knitted Jacquard Mesh. In contrast, Dual Layer Knitted Jacquard Mesh consists of two interconnected fabric layers, offering enhanced durability, insulation, and support, making it suitable for high-performance athletic gear and specialized industrial applications. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
