<p><ul><li>Single Beam</li><li>Multi-beam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electron Beam Inspection System (EBIS) market is categorized into two primary types: Single Beam and Multi-Beam systems. Single Beam systems utilize a single electron beam for scanning, offering high-resolution imaging ideal for detailed defect inspection in semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, Multi-Beam systems employ multiple electron beams simultaneously, enhancing throughput and efficiency by enabling faster inspection times and larger area coverage. This differentiation addresses various industry needs, balancing resolution, speed, and cost-effectiveness in advanced electronic manufacturing processes.</p></p>
