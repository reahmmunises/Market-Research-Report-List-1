<p><ul><li>Dynamic Image Analysis</li><li>Static Image Analysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the powder flowability testers market, dynamic image analysis and static image analysis are the two primary types. Dynamic image analysis measures the flow behavior of powders during movement, capturing real-time data on particle size and shape as they flow, providing insights into processing performance. In contrast, static image analysis evaluates the particle characteristics while at rest, offering a snapshot of bulk powder properties. Both techniques are essential for optimizing powder handling and processing in industries like pharmaceuticals, food, and cosmetics.</p></p>
