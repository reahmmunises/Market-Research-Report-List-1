<p><strong>The Wound Measurement Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wound measurement devices market is divided into two primary types: contact and non-contact. Contact type devices involve direct physical interaction with the wound, utilizing tools like rulers or digital gauges to measure dimensions accurately. These devices can provide precise measurements but may cause discomfort. Non-contact type devices, on the other hand, use imaging technologies, such as lasers or infrared sensors, to assess wound size without physical contact, offering a pain-free alternative and reducing the risk of infection.</p></p>
