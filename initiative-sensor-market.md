<p><p>The initiative sensor market can be categorized into two main types: Single Beam Type and Multi-beam Type sensors. Single Beam Type sensors utilize a single beam to detect objects or measure distances, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. In contrast, Multi-beam Type sensors employ multiple beams to cover a broader area, offering higher accuracy and better detection capabilities. These advanced sensors excel in complex environments where precise measurements and object differentiation are crucial for applications in automation, robotics, and navigation.</p></p>
