<p><p>Exhaust Gas Temperature (EGT) sensors play a crucial role in monitoring the temperature of exhaust gases in engines, helping optimize performance and reduce emissions. The market for EGT sensors primarily features two types: PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) sensors, which increase resistance with temperature, providing precise measurements in high-temperature conditions, and NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) sensors, which decrease resistance as temperature rises, offering rapid response times. Both types are essential for effective engine management and compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
