<p><p>The 5G cellular baseband chip market is primarily categorized into two types: single-mode chips and multi-mode chips. Single-mode chips are designed to support only one type of network technology, typically focusing on 5G. In contrast, multi-mode chips can operate across various networks, including 4G, 3G, and 5G, providing greater flexibility and compatibility for devices. This multi-mode capability allows manufacturers to cater to diverse market needs and enhance user experiences by enabling seamless connectivity across different network generations.</p></p>
