<p><p>PA/GA Systems encompass various market types, including Traditional Pressure Broadcasting and Network Broadcasting Systems. Traditional Pressure Broadcasting relies on analog methods for distributing public address communications, often using centralized speaker systems for announcements. In contrast, Network Broadcasting Systems leverage digital technology, allowing for real-time, scalable communication over IP networks. This modern approach enhances flexibility and integration with other systems, enabling more effective and efficient dissemination of information in diverse environments, such as large venues or complex facilities.</p></p>
