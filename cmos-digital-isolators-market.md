<p><p>The CMOS digital isolators market is primarily segmented into two types: unidirectional and bidirectional digital isolators. Unidirectional digital isolators transmit signals in one direction, providing robust isolation and are ideal for applications requiring strict data flow control, such as power supply systems. In contrast, bidirectional digital isolators facilitate bidirectional communication, allowing data to pass in both directions, which is beneficial for applications needing real-time feedback and communication, such as in industrial automation and communication systems.</p></p>
