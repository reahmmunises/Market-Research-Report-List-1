<p><p>The Mine and IED Detection Systems market encompasses three primary technologies: sensor-based, radar-based, and laser-based systems. Sensor-based systems utilize advanced detection technologies, such as ground-penetrating radar and metal detectors, to identify buried threats. Radar-based systems employ electromagnetic waves to detect anomalies in terrain and pinpoint mines or IEDs. Laser-based systems use optical sensors to detect explosive materials by analyzing light patterns. Together, these technologies enhance safety and effectiveness in military and civilian demining operations.</p></p>
