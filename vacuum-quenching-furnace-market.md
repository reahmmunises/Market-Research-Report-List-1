<p><strong>The Vacuum Quenching Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The vacuum quenching furnace market is segmented primarily into gas quenching and liquid quenching types. Gas quenching employs inert gases, like nitrogen or argon, to cool materials rapidly, minimizing oxidation and distortion, making it suitable for delicate components. In contrast, liquid quenching utilizes a quenching medium, such as oil or polymer solutions, providing faster cooling rates and is often used for robust parts. Each method offers distinct advantages based on material properties and specific application needs.</p></p>
