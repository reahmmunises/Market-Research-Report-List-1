<p><p>Camera fixed neutral density (ND) filters are categorized into types based on their mounting methods. Screw-in filters attach directly to the lens thread, providing a straightforward solution for light reduction. Insertion filters fit into a holder mounted on the lens, allowing for easy changes. Clamping filters attach using a clamp mechanism, ensuring a secure fit without threading. Other markets may include specialized filters or those designed for specific camera systems, catering to diverse photographic needs and preferences.</p></p>
