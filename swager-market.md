<p><p>The swaging machine market is primarily segmented into radial and rotary swaging machines. Radial swaging machines utilize a series of hammers to compress material outward, suitable for producing complex shapes and achieving precise tolerances. In contrast, rotary swaging machines employ a rotating die mechanism that efficiently shapes cylindrical components with high speed and reduced material waste. Both types cater to different industry needs, including aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, effectively enhancing production efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
