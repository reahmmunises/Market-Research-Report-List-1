<p><p>The Hydroprocessing Catalyst (HPC) market encompasses two primary types: hydrotreating and hydrocracking. Hydrotreating focuses on removing impurities such as sulfur and nitrogen from petroleum products, enhancing their quality and environmental compliance. In contrast, hydrocracking breaks down larger, heavier hydrocarbons into lighter, more valuable fuels, improving yield and efficiency. Both processes are crucial in refining operations, driving demand for specialized catalysts that optimize performance, increase throughput, and meet stringent regulatory standards in the petroleum industry.</p></p>
