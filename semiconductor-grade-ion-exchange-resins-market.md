<p><ul><li>Anion Ion-exchange Resin</li><li>Cation Ion-exchange Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor grade ion exchange resins market is primarily divided into two types: anion ion-exchange resins and cation ion-exchange resins. Anion resins are designed to selectively remove negatively charged ions from solutions, playing a vital role in ultra-pure water production for semiconductor manufacturing. Cation resins, on the other hand, target positively charged ions, effectively softening water and enhancing its purity. Both types are critical for achieving the stringent quality requirements essential in semiconductor fabrication processes.</p></p>
