<p><ul><li>Plastic Pancreatic Stents</li><li>Metal Pancreatic Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pancreatic stent market comprises two main types: plastic and metal pancreatic stents. Plastic pancreatic stents are flexible, cost-effective, and commonly used for short-term drainage in patients with conditions like pancreatitis. They offer a simpler removal process but may have a higher risk of occlusion over time. Metal pancreatic stents, on the other hand, are more durable and designed for long-term use, providing greater resistance to clogging, making them suitable for patients requiring prolonged management of pancreatic duct obstructions.</p></p>
