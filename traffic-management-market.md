<p><p>The Traffic Management market encompasses various advanced systems aimed at optimizing urban mobility. Urban Traffic Management and Control (UTMC) systems facilitate real-time traffic flow management. Adaptive Traffic Control Systems (ATCS) adjust signal timings based on live traffic conditions. Journey Time Measurement Systems (JTMS) provide data on travel times for route optimization. Predictive Traffic Modeling Systems (PTMS) forecast traffic patterns for proactive planning. Incident Detection and Location Systems (IDLS) identify and pinpoint traffic incidents, while Dynamic Traffic Management Systems (DTMS) implement real-time strategies to manage congestion and improve safety.</p></p>
