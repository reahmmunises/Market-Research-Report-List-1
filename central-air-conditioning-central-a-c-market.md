<p><p>Central air conditioning systems provide efficient cooling for large spaces by utilizing a central unit to distribute conditioned air through ducts. The split system consists of an indoor evaporator and an outdoor condenser, allowing flexible installation and quieter operation. In contrast, packaged units combine all components in a single outdoor unit, making them space-saving and ideal for commercial applications. Both types effectively regulate indoor climate but cater to different installation preferences and space requirements.</p></p>
