<p><p>The automotive aftermarket glass market is primarily segmented into tempered glass, laminated glass, and others. Tempered glass is heat-treated for enhanced strength and safety, commonly used for side and rear windows. Laminated glass consists of two glass layers bonded with a plastic interlayer, offering superior safety by preventing shattering and minimizing UV exposure, often utilized in windshields. The "others" category includes specialty glass types such as privacy, solar control, and digital displays, catering to diverse consumer preferences and vehicle designs.</p></p>
