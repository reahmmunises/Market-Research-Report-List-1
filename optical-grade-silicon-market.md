<p><strong>The Optical Grade Silicon Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski (CZ) Method</li><li>Float Zone (FZ) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical grade silicon market encompasses two primary production methods: the Czochralski (CZ) method and the Float Zone (FZ) method. The CZ method involves drawing a silicon crystal from molten silicon, producing large, single-crystal ingots suitable for optical applications. In contrast, the FZ method utilizes a floating zone technique to create high-purity silicon without contaminants, ideal for advanced optical systems. Both methods cater to different performance requirements and applications, influencing market dynamics in optical components and devices.</p></p>
