<p><strong>The Portable Piezometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Piezometers</li><li>Hydraulic Piezometers</li><li>Pneumatic Piezometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable piezometers market is segmented into three main types: electric piezometers, hydraulic piezometers, and pneumatic piezometers. Electric piezometers utilize sensors to measure groundwater levels and pressure, offering high accuracy and quick data capture. Hydraulic piezometers employ tubes filled with liquid to monitor pressure changes, often in open environments. Pneumatic piezometers, on the other hand, measure pressure through air pressure in a gas-filled tube. Each type serves diverse applications in environmental monitoring, construction, and hydrological studies, meeting specific needs for accuracy and portability.</p></p>
