<p><p>The Hermetic Grain Storage market consists of various types designed to preserve grain quality and prevent spoilage. Bag storage involves airtight bags that limit air exchange, ideal for small-scale farmers. Cocoon storage offers a larger, flexible, and tubular option for bulk grains, effectively sealing out moisture and pests. Container storage utilizes standard shipping containers for efficient transport and protection. Lastly, bunker storage provides large, sealed structures suitable for high-capacity storage, ensuring long-term preservation of grain while minimizing losses.</p></p>
