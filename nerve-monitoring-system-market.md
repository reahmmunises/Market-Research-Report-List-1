<p><ul><li>Nerve Monitor</li><li>Nerve Stimulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nerve Monitoring System market consists of two primary types: nerve monitors and nerve stimulators. Nerve monitors are devices that continuously track nerve functions during surgical procedures, enhancing patient safety by minimizing nerve damage. In contrast, nerve stimulators are used to activate specific nerves, aiding in identifying their location and function during surgeries. Together, these tools provide critical intraoperative information, enabling surgeons to make informed decisions and improve surgical outcomes while reducing complications associated with nerve injury.</p></p>
