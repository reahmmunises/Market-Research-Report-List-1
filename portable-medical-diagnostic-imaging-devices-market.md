Ultrasound utilizes sound waves for real-time imaging, making it ideal for soft tissue examination, particularly in obstetrics. Endoscopes allow for direct visualization inside the body through a flexible tube, facilitating diagnostics and procedures. Each type enhances accessibility and efficiency in medical settings.</p></p>
